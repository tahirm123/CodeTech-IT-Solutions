Password Policy

Effective Date: 10th February 2024

1. Purpose
   The purpose of this password policy is to ensure the security of company information systems by enforcing the use of strong and unique passwords.

2. Password Creation and Complexity
   2.1 Password Length
       - Passwords must be a minimum of 12 characters.
   2.2 Character Types
       - Passwords must include characters from at least three of the following categories:
         - Uppercase letters (A-Z)
         - Lowercase letters (a-z)
         - Numbers (0-9)
         - Special characters (e.g., !, @, #, $, %)

3. Password Management
   3.1 Unique Passwords
       - Users are prohibited from using the same password for multiple accounts.
   3.2 Password Expiration
       - Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
   3.3 Password History
       - The system will remember and prevent the reuse of the last 5 passwords.
   3.4 Account Lockout
       - After 5 consecutive unsuccessful login attempts, the account will be locked for a duration of 15 minutes.

4. Password Storage
   4.1 Password Storage
       - Passwords must be stored securely using industry-standard encryption methods.
   4.2 Plain Text Passwords
       - Storing passwords in plain text is strictly prohibited.

5. User Responsibilities
   5.1 Password Confidentiality
       - Users must keep their passwords confidential and must not share them with anyone, including IT support personnel.
   5.2 Reporting
       - Users must promptly report any suspected or actual unauthorized use of their account.

6. Password Reset
   6.1 Verification
       - Password reset requests will be subject to a secure verification process to confirm the identity of the user.

7. Non-Compliance
   7.1 Violations
       - Violations of this password policy may result in disciplinary action, including account suspension or termination.

8. Review and Revision
   8.1 Periodic Review
       - This password policy will be reviewed periodically and revised as necessary to adapt to changing security requirements.

By adhering to this password policy, employees contribute to the overall security of the company's information systems.
